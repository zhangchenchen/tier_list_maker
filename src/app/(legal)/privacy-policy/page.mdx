# Privacy Policy for Tier List Maker

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to **Tier List Maker** (tierlist-maker.com), the free online tool for creating and sharing tier lists. At Tier List Maker, your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our service.

## Information We Collect

### 1. **Local Storage Data**

- **What We Collect**: Your tier list data (uploaded images and tier arrangements) is stored locally in your browser using localStorage.
- **Purpose**: To auto-save your work and restore it when you return to the site.
- **Important**: This data never leaves your device and is not sent to our servers.

### 2. **Uploaded Images**

- **What We Collect**: Images you upload to create your tier lists.
- **Purpose**: To display in your tier list and include in exported images.
- **Important**: All image processing happens in your browser. We do not store or transmit your images to any server.

### 3. **Analytics Data (Optional)**

- **What We Collect**: Anonymous usage statistics such as page views, button clicks, and feature usage.
- **Purpose**: To understand how users interact with Tier List Maker and improve our service.
- **Important**: We do not collect personally identifiable information.

### 4. **Cookies**

- **What We Collect**: Essential cookies for site functionality and preferences (theme, language).
- **Purpose**: To remember your preferences and enhance user experience.
- **Third-party**: We may use analytics cookies from services like Google Analytics or Plausible.

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Processing**: All tier list creation happens entirely in your browser. Your images and tier arrangements are processed locally using JavaScript.
- **No Server Storage**: We do not store your tier list data, uploaded images, or personal information on our servers.
- **LocalStorage**: Data is stored in your browser's localStorage, which is private to your device and browser.
- **Export**: When you export your tier list as an image, it's generated in your browser and downloaded directly to your device.

## Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

- **Analytics Services** (e.g., Google Analytics, Plausible): For anonymous usage statistics
- **CDN Services** (e.g., Vercel, Cloudflare): For hosting and delivering our website
- **Payment Processors** (if applicable): For processing donations or premium features (future)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

## Your Rights and Control

Since we don't collect or store personal data:

- **Your Data Stays Local**: All your tier list data remains in your browser's localStorage
- **Delete Anytime**: Use the "Delete All" button or clear your browser data to remove all tier list data
- **No Account Required**: You can use Tier List Maker without creating an account
- **Export Control**: You have complete control over when and how you export your tier lists

## Children's Privacy

Tier List Maker does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Our service can be used without providing any personal information.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

## Cookies and Tracking

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of Tier List Maker.
